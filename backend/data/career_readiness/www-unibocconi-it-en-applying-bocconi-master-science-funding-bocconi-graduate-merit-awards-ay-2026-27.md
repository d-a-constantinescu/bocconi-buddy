# Bocconi Graduate Merit Awards a.y. 2026-27

The official Bocconi page for Graduate Merit Awards a.y. 2026-27 says the key information is a 100% waiver on the Bocconi ordinary academic tuition and fees.

The page states that Merit Awards are available for first year Graduate applicants for the 2026-2027 academic year based on their academic standing.

The Merit Award includes a full tuition and fees waiver.

The page describes the award as a tuition and fees waiver. It does not describe a separate cash stipend.
