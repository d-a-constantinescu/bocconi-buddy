# Master of Science fees a.y. 2026-27

The official Bocconi fees page says that, for the 2026-27 academic year, students enrolled in their first year of a Master of Science program have tuition and fees at Bocconi set at 18,550 EUR per year.

The same page lists 20% partial tuition waiver total due of 14,840 EUR and 60% partial tuition waiver total due of 7,420 EUR.
