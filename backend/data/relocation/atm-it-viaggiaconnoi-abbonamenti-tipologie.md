# ATM Milan urban subscription fares

The official ATM fare page lists ordinary urban subscriptions for travel only in Milan:

- ordinary monthly urban subscription: 39 EUR
- ordinary annual urban subscription: 330 EUR, valid for 12 consecutive calendar months

The same page lists the urban youth subscription up to 27 years:

- for students and workers up to the age of 27
- monthly: 22 EUR
- annual: 200 EUR, valid for 12 consecutive calendar months
